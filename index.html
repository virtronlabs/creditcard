The Esports Athletic Commission (EAC) is Virtron's official regulatory body dedicated to overseeing and regulating various esports, with a primary focus on combat sports such as boxing and mixed martial arts within the metaverse.

The commission plays a pivotal role in licensing, supervising stakeholders, and ensuring the fair and ethical conduct of unarmed combat esports.

The Association of Sanctioning Bodies Commission is comprised of several championship title bodies. Virtron Virtual Sanctioning (VVS); The Project behind the Virtron Belt or VVS Belt, is considered the most sought after and respected title in the world of virtual combat sports.

Choose Your Role

Step into the world of virtual combat sports in any way you choose. Manage fighters, promote events, train champions, or enter the ring yourself. The power is in your hands!

Promoter

Bring the crowd and make the biggest matchups happen. Get your own dedicated Promoter Dashboard to manage social media, streams, payments, and all the crucial data you need to run the show.
